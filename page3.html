Fun Facts

  • Name Meaning: "Alisa" is the Russian form of "Alice," reflecting her Russian origin.
  • Modeled After: She was designed in the image of Dr. Bosconovitch's late daughter.
  • Hidden Weapons: Her body houses chainsaws, rocket boosters, explosive head, and detachable fists.
  • Fan Favorite: Alisa is consistently ranked among the most popular Tekken characters worldwide.
  • Personality: Despite being a robot, she displays genuine emotions like kindness, curiosity, and loyalty.
  • Cross-Over: Alisa has appeared in other games such as Street Fighter X Tekken and Project X Zone.
